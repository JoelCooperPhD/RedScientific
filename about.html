About

Road safety through research, education, and device development

Joel Cooper

Joel Cooper founded Red Scientific to bridge the gap between academic research and practical tools for measuring human attention. With a background in human factors and cognitive science, Joel has focused his career on understanding driver distraction and developing methods to measure it reliably.

His work spans device development, research methodology, and applied studies with industry partners. The goal: safer roads through better understanding of human attention.

Mission

Red Scientific exists to advance road safety through three interconnected activities:

  • Research — Conducting applied studies that generate actionable insights about driver attention and distraction
  • Education — Sharing knowledge through publications, presentations, and collaboration with academic and industry partners
  • Device Development — Building practical, reliable tools that enable other researchers to measure attention accurately

Approach

We believe good research requires good tools. That's why we don't just conduct studies—we build the instruments needed to do the work properly. Our devices are designed by researchers, for researchers, with an emphasis on precision, ease of use, and adherence to international standards.

We work with automotive manufacturers, government agencies, and university research labs to tackle real-world questions about driver attention and vehicle safety.
