Precision Instruments

We develop research devices designed for precision, reliability, and compliance with international standards. Our tools implement the ISO and NHTSA protocols used worldwide for driver distraction assessment.

Hardware

Visual Occlusion Goggles

ISO 16673 and NHTSA compliant goggles for measuring visual demand. Electronically controlled lenses alternate between transparent and opaque states to quantify how much visual attention in-vehicle tasks require.

Compliance

Standards We Support

ISO 16673:2007

Occlusion method for assessing visual demand of in-vehicle systems. Our goggles implement the 1.5-second interval timing specified in the standard.

ISO 17488:2016

Detection Response Task methodology for cognitive load measurement. We offer all three stimulus modalities defined in the standard.

NHTSA Guidelines

Visual-Manual Driver Distraction Guidelines for in-vehicle electronic devices. Our equipment supports the acceptance testing protocols.
