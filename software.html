Logger

Synchronized multi-sensor data collection for automotive and human factors research.

Professional Data Acquisition for Raspberry Pi

Logger is a modular data collection platform designed for researchers who need synchronized capture of video, audio, eye tracking, and behavioral task data. All modules record with frame-level synchronization (~30ms accuracy) and export timestamped CSV data for analysis.

7 Modules Cameras, Audio, Eye Tracker, DRT, VOG, GPS, Notes
Synchronized Frame-level timing across all data streams
Portable Battery-powered operation for field research
